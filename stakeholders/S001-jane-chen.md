## Background

Example stakeholder attached to the example project. Jane founded Acme
Holdings 8 years ago, owns 51% of the company. She has final authority on
any purchase above €10K. Has worked with two prior software vendors, one
positive experience and one negative.

## What she cares about

- Saving her team time on manual portfolio tracking
- Data portability — she wants the ability to export and leave
- Staff training time being minimal

## What she worries about

- Vendor going out of business and leaving her stranded
- Her property manager struggling with a new tool
- Hidden fees or scope creep
