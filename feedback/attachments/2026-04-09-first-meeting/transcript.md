# Voice Agent Interview — 2026-04-09
# Session: first-meeting | Client: Acme Holdings CEO | Agent: voice-agent-v2

---

**Agent:** Thank you for joining today. Can you start by describing what you
do with your properties day-to-day?

**Client:** Sure. I own twelve buildings in Sofia — apartments mostly, a
couple of mixed-use. Right now I track everything in Excel. One sheet per
building, another one that I update manually to get the overall picture.
It works, but it breaks every time I add a new property.

**Agent:** What does "breaks" mean in practice?

**Client:** Formulas stop updating, I forget to copy a row, someone sends
me a file and it's a different format. Last month I had to spend half a day
reconciling because my accountant had a different number for occupancy.

**Agent:** What would you want to see when you open the app in the morning?

**Client:** Just a summary. All my buildings, how many apartments each has,
how many are rented out, how much I'm collecting this month. Like a control
panel.

**Agent:** How do you currently add a new building to your records?

**Client:** I create a new Excel sheet, copy the template, fill in the
address and the apartment list. For a big building that's an hour of work.
It would be amazing if I could just upload a spreadsheet I already have.

**Agent:** How many apartments does a typical building have?

**Client:** It varies a lot. My smallest is three apartments — a converted
house. My biggest is 133. And most are something in between, like 28 or 47.
Nothing round.

**Agent:** Any other people involved in managing the properties?

**Client:** Not right now, it's just me. Well, there's someone who handles
the day-to-day for two of the buildings, but that's informal.

**Agent:** What happens when a tenant is late with rent?

**Client:** I call them, or they call me. I'd love to just do it through the
app — send a reminder, collect payment. But that's probably more complicated.

**Agent:** Understood. Any features you know you don't need?

**Client:** I don't need anything fancy. No reports to print, no client
portals. Just a tool my team and I can use to stay on top of things.
